Meet Our Staff

Mr McMaster Principal

As the principal of Drumahoe Primary School Mr McMaster and the Board of Governors have full responsibly for the management of Kadet Klub.

Heather Glenn Bursar/Manager

As the Bursar/Manager Heather has a responsibility for the daily running of Kadet Klub.

Tracy Lovell Play Leader

"Working at Kadet Klub is challenging but rewarding. It's great to see the kids having fun in a relaxed atmosphere. It makes it worthwhile when you can see a smile on their faces. At Kadet Klub the staff work well together and this also makes my job enjoyable. Not only is it about the kids but it's great getting to know the parents too and to know that their trust is in us to care for their children."

Rebecca Watson Play Worker

"I enjoy working in Kadet Klub as no two days are the same. I enjoy working with the children and watching them grow from P1-P7. I find it rewarding to see the children develop socially as they make new friends with their peers and come out of their shells through the activities planned. Kadet Klub is enjoyable, meeting new people all the time."

Katherine McCandless Play Worker

"Everyday I enjoy working at Kadet Klub as kids are a lot of fun to work with. Their honesty and sense of humour make it all worthwhile. Watching each child learn and grow differently as they progress through the school can be very rewarding."

Lisa Curry Play Worker

"I like working in Kadet Klub as it is a warm and friendly environment, where all staff work together in a very positive way. On a daily basis there are a range of children who look forward to the different activities planned for them. All children have their own unique personality, and within Kadet Klub they all get the opportunity to express this through art, music and play. I look forward to Kadet Klub everyday as there are different children who attend, and always tell me funny stories about what happened them at home or in class."

Phoebe Love Play Worker

"I enjoy working with the children in Kadet Klub. The activities are planned to include all abilities and age groups which gives each child a sense of belonging."

Lauren Miller Play Worker

"Working with the pupils in Kadet Klub is very rewarding. As I get to know the pupils, I get to see their individual character which can be totally different in the relaxed atmosphere of Kadet Klub."
